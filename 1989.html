Considering the great success of her previous album, Taylor felt immense pressure to one up herself. Critics tried to undercut Taylor’s songwriting abilities, claiming her praise was invalid because she sometimes worked with co-writers. This fueled Taylor to write Speak Now ALL BY HERSELF; not a single person helped her write the album. The infamous 2009 VMAs incident where Kanye West interrupted Taylor also pushed her to prove that she was deserving of her place in the music industry.
