Composed of songs written about topics that keep Taylor up at night, Midnights touches on the notions of anxiety, insecurity, and self-awareness. In terms of sound, the album incorporates chill-out and dream pop styles. Taylor revealed the tracklist over a number of weeks via TikTok where she spun a bingo cage and revealed the title of the track whose track number correlated with the number on the ball.
