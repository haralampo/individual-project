"reputation" was a bold departure from Taylor’s previous musical styles, marked by a darker and more edgy tone. This album was the result of an online incident in July of 2016 where Taylor’s character was slandered on the basis of false claims, causing a snowball effect of hate comments and Taylor being “canceled.” After this, Taylor disappeared and no one physically saw her for a year. In August of 2017, Taylor wiped every photo from her Instagram and began teasing her new album by posting graphics of snakes - note that her comment sections for the past year had been flooded with snake emojis.

The album, characterized by its electro-pop and synth-heavy sound, explored themes of public perception, media scrutiny, personal growth, and reclaiming one's narrative. She maintained a relatively low profile, engaging with fans through selective social media posts and surprise interactions. Coining the phrase “there will be no explanation, there will just be reputation,” Taylor did no interviews during this era. Despite a more subdued public presence, the Reputation Stadium Tour was a spectacle and broke the record for the highest grossing U.S. tour.
