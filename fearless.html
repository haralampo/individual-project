Taylor’s sophomore album, "Fearless", allowed her to reach mainstream audiences, gaining recognition outside of the country music scene. This country pop album spawned the hits “Love Story” and “You Belong With Me.” Her growing fame allowed her to take advantage of opportunities even outside of music, including playing a role in the movie Valentine’s Day and hosting Saturday Night Live.

This critically acclaimed album received great recognition and praise. "Fearless" won the Grammys’ biggest honor, Album of the Year, and Taylor was the youngest person to win the award at the time; she won three other Grammys that night. To date, "Fearless" is the most awarded country album of all time.
